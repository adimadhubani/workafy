1. Introduction

Welcome to Workafy — a platform that connects companies, recruiters, freelancers, and job seekers. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

By using our platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you disagree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, phone number, and user type (recruiter, freelancer, or job seeker).
  • Profile Details: Skills, experience, education, certifications, resume/CV, and profile picture.
  • Company Details: Company name, description, recruiter info, and job postings.
  • Payment Details: Processed via secure third-party payment gateways.
  • Communication Data: Chats, messages, and feedback shared through the platform.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device details, browser type, and IP address.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, clicks, and time spent.
  • Cookies and tracking tools to improve performance and personalization.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To connect freelancers, recruiters, and job seekers.
  • To process secure payments and transactions.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To send notifications, updates, and support messages.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Recruiters and Employers: When you apply for a job or project.
  • Freelancers or Job Seekers: When companies post or contact candidates.
  • Third-Party Services: Like payment processors and analytics providers.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect Workafy’s rights.

We never sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Workafy uses cookies to enhance your experience by remembering preferences and analyzing usage patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely with modern encryption and firewall protections. We use SSL encryption for all transactions. However, we encourage users to keep their passwords confidential and log out after each session.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Opt out of promotional emails.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@workafy.com.

8. Payment Information

Workafy uses secure, PCI-DSS-compliant payment gateways. We do not store full payment details — all transactions are encrypted and processed safely.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Once it’s no longer required, we securely delete or anonymize it.

10. Children’s Privacy

Workafy is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has shared data with us, contact us for immediate removal.

11. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last Updated” date. Please review this page regularly to stay informed.
