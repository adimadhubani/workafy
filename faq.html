Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have

Account & Registration

How do I create an account on JobPortal?

Creating an account on JobPortal is simple. Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right of any page, fill in your details including name, email address, and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can complete your profile and start applying for jobs.

Is there a fee to create an account?

No, creating a job seeker account on JobPortal is completely free. You can create a profile, upload your resume, search for jobs, and apply to positions without any charges. Some premium features may require payment, but the basic functionality is free for all users.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Login" button and then select "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.

Job Search & Applications

How do I search for jobs on JobPortal?

You can search for jobs using the search bar at the top of any page. Enter keywords, job titles, or company names. You can also use filters to narrow down results by location, salary range, experience level, job type (full-time, part-time, contract), and industry. Save your search criteria to get notified when new matching jobs are posted.

How do I apply for a job?

When you find a job you're interested in, click on the job title to view the full description. If you want to apply, click the "Apply Now" button. You can use your existing resume or upload a new one. Some employers may require additional questions or assessments as part of the application process.

Can I track my job applications?

Yes, JobPortal provides an application tracker in your dashboard. You can see all the jobs you've applied for, their current status (submitted, viewed, interview, rejected, etc.), and any updates from employers. You'll also receive email notifications when there are changes to your application status.

For Employers

How do I post a job on JobPortal?

Employers can post jobs by creating a company account. Click on "For Employers" and then "Post a Job." You'll need to provide details about the position including job title, description, requirements, location, and salary range. Once submitted, our team will review and approve your job posting, usually within 24 hours.

What are the pricing plans for employers?

JobPortal offers several pricing tiers for employers. Our Basic plan allows for up to 3 job postings per month. The Professional plan offers unlimited job postings and advanced candidate filtering. The Enterprise plan includes premium features like featured job slots, advanced analytics, and dedicated account management. Contact our sales team for custom enterprise solutions.

How can I search for candidates?

Employers with active job postings can access our candidate database. Use filters to search by skills, experience, education, location, and more. You can save candidate searches and set up alerts for when new candidates matching your criteria join JobPortal. Advanced search features are available in our Professional and Enterprise plans.

Privacy & Security

Is my personal information safe on JobPortal?

Yes, we take data privacy and security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal information. Your contact information is never shared with employers without your permission. You can control your privacy settings in your account dashboard, including whether your profile is visible to employers and what information they can see.

Can I make my profile private?

Absolutely. In your account settings, you can choose to make your profile completely private, which means employers won't be able to find you in searches. You can also choose a semi-private setting where only employers you apply to can view your profile. Your current employer will never see that you're looking for new opportunities unless you choose to share that information.

How do I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your account, go to your account settings and select "Delete Account." Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including applications, saved jobs, and profile information will be permanently removed from our systems. If you're facing issues, consider contacting our support team first - we might be able to help resolve your concerns.
