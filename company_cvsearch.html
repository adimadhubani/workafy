Frequently Asked Questions

How does the CV search work?
Our CV search allows you to find candidates based on skills, experience, education, location, and more. You can use advanced filters to narrow down your search to find the perfect candidates for your job openings.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period, and you won't be charged again.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Is there a free trial available?
We offer a 7-day free trial for our Professional and Enterprise plans. During the trial, you'll have full access to all features with some usage limits.
